    Professional bio¶

    I have extensive knowledge and experience in cyber security, information technology, and digital transformation. I use my expertise to ensure that businesses closely align their technology stack with their business strategies. My colleagues, clients, and senior executives, such as CIOs and CTOs, acknowledge my technical and business acumen in advancing cyber security within their diverse business supporting technologies, including IT, cloud, data, and server architectures.

    These skills helped me start two cyber security companies that design and build cyber security solutions for a wide range of businesses. The companies have successfully provided a diverse range of cyber services to financial institutions, national critical infrastructure agencies, manufacturing, and R&D enterprises. This particular activity helps me realise my dream of being a prime mover in the information technology discipline.

    Opportunities to share my experience within the information security field include presenting at conferences, authoring cyber security articles, and editing information security publications. Moreover, these endeavours underpin a strongly held conviction that nurturing young talent to become the next generation of experts is the best way to retain a thriving industry.
